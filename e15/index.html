  2. Draw a circle sized 100px.
  3. Give the circle a fill of # and stroke of # with weight 1px.
  4. Position the circle in the center.
  5. Draw a rectangle with a height of 500px and width of 10px. Give it a fill of #.
  6. Position it at the right of the square artboard.
  7. Draw a rectangle with a height of 500px and width of 10px. Give it a fill of #.
  8. Position it at the left of the square artboard.
  9. Draw a rectangle with a height of 500px and width of 10px. Give it a fill of #.
  10. Position it at the top of the square artboard.
  11. Draw a rectangle with a height of 500px and width of 10px. Give it a fill of #.
  12. Position it at the bottom of the square artboard.
